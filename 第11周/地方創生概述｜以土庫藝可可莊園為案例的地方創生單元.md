## 課程大綱： 以土庫藝可可莊園為案例的地方創生單元

### 單元一：臺灣地方創生的背景與脈絡

Unit 1: Background of Taiwan’s Local Revitalization

* 2019 年啟動「地方創生國家戰略計畫」，目標是回應人口外流、城鄉失衡等問題。
  In 2019, Taiwan launched the National Local Revitalization Strategy to tackle depopulation and urban–rural imbalance.

* COVID-19 對以產業發展為核心的創生計畫造成重大衝擊，但也促使團隊與社區系統更緊密連結。
  COVID-19 heavily impacted industry-centered projects, but also pushed teams to connect more deeply with local communities.

* 創生場域多元：農村、漁村、原鄉、離島等各地「百花齊放」。
  Revitalization now appears in various places: farming villages, fishing villages, indigenous areas, offshore islands, etc.

* 主體從「青年返鄉」擴展為「全齡共創」，不同世代一起面對地方問題。
  The main actors evolved from “youth returning home” to “all generations co-creating solutions.”

---

### 單元二：案例地點與在地問題——雲林土庫鎮

Unit 2: The Case Location & Local Issues – Tuku Township, Yunlin

* 土庫老街長期沒落，老店歇業、建築閒置，商圈失去活力。
  Tuku Old Street had long declined, with closed shops and idle buildings.

* 土庫鎮人口不到三萬人，高齡人口比例高，65 歲以上達 21.76%。
  The township has fewer than 30,000 residents and a high aging rate (21.76% over 65).

* 人口外流、老化與產業傳統保守，是地方發展的主要挑戰。
  Depopulation, aging, and conservative traditional industries are key challenges.

---

### 單元三：創辦人返鄉契機與土地再生

Unit 3: Founder’s Return and Land Regeneration

* 創辦人陳銀豪因父親失智、母親照顧壓力過大而返鄉，決定全職照顧家人。
  Founder Chen Yin-hao returned home to care for his father with dementia and his overwhelmed mother.

* 購入 1.5 公頃農地，發現其前身是廢棄養豬場與地下毒品交易場域，嚴重污染、惡臭四溢。
  He bought 1.5 hectares of farmland that turned out to be an abandoned pig farm and former drug site, severely polluted.

* 堅決註銷價值上千萬的養豬執照，投入約五千萬元進行整地、填土與環境修復。
  He cancelled a valuable pig-farming license (worth ~NTD 10M) and spent ~NTD 50M on remediation.

* 從零開始學農：就讀農民大學、雲科大有機專班、農委會課程，累積農業知識。
  With no agricultural background, he studied at farmers’ school, YunTech organic programs, and COA courses.

---

### 單元四：可可產業導入與 Tree-to-Bar 商業模式

Unit 4: Introducing Cocoa Industry & Tree-to-Bar Business Model

* 選擇種植可可：兼具園藝治療功能與預防失智、心血管疾病等健康價值。
  He chose cocoa for both horticultural therapy and its health benefits (dementia, cardiovascular health).

* 建立「Tree to Bar（從樹到吧台）」一條龍模式：
  栽種 → 採果 → 剖果 → 發酵 → 日曬 → 烘焙 → 研磨 → 巧克力產品。
  He built a “Tree to Bar” model: from planting trees to harvesting, fermenting, drying, roasting, grinding, and chocolate making.

* 堅持使用純可可脂、無化學添加，成本為一般 2–5 倍，定位為頂級天然巧克力。
  He insists on pure cocoa butter and no additives, with 2–5 times the usual cost, positioned as premium natural chocolate.

* 與雲林出身的國際巧克力大師李宙希合作，獲其擔任技術總監。
  He partnered with international chocolate master Lee Chou-hsi as technical director.

* 2023 年獲得 TICCA 台灣可可精品豆評鑑冠軍，提升品牌與地方能見度。
  In 2023, they won the TICCA Taiwan fine cocoa bean award, boosting brand and local visibility.

---

### 單元五：園區設計、食農教育與觀光體驗

Unit 5: Park Design, Agri-food Education & Tourism

* 智慧溫室與玻璃屋：做為可可食農教育場域與體驗空間。
  Smart greenhouses and glasshouses are used for cocoa agri-food education and experiences.

* 園區作物多元：可可、美生菜、香菜等，結合餐飲空間，提供從產地到餐桌的體驗。
  The park grows cocoa, lettuce, coriander, etc., linking to restaurant spaces for “farm-to-table” experiences.

* 規劃可可加工廠、巧克力學院等，讓遊客能完整了解巧克力產製流程。
  A cocoa processing plant and chocolate academy show visitors the full production process.

* 假日兩天可吸引上千名遊客，包含散客、社團深度旅遊與旅行社團體。
  On weekends, thousands of visitors come: individual tourists, group tours, and travel agencies.

---

### 單元六：商圈串聯與社區共好

Unit 6: Business District Linkage & Community Co-benefits

* 帶動土庫老街觀光人潮，假日甚至出現塞車，政公所改為煩惱停車問題。
  The park drives traffic to Tuku Old Street; on holidays, traffic jams occur and parking becomes the main concern.

* 返鄉青年與老店二、三代共同成立「土庫好庫商圈發展協會」，串聯店家、辦活動。
  Returning youth and 2nd–3rd gen shop owners formed the Tuku Good Business Association to organize events and link shops.

* 以可可莊園為亮點，導流遊客至周邊產業：農會、倉庫樂米工坊、鳥蛋加工廠、圓順芝麻觀光油廠等。
  Using the cocoa manor as a highlight, visitors are funneled to nearby attractions: farmers’ association, rice-bread factory, quail-egg plant, sesame oil plant, etc.

* 小鎮經濟以「亮點 × 串聯」模式翻轉，創造更多年輕人願意留下或返鄉的條件。
  The small town economy is revived through a “flagship + linkage” model, encouraging more youth to stay or return.

---

### 單元七：地方創生的啟示與反思

Unit 7: Lessons & Reflections on Local Revitalization

* 創生是一場「沒有期程的長遠行動」，改變不一定立刻看見。
  Local revitalization is a long-term process without a fixed end date; change is gradual.

* 從「一座可可莊園」出發，帶動土地修復、產業轉型、人口返鄉與社區組織再生。
  One cocoa estate catalyzed land restoration, industrial transformation, return migration, and new community organizations.

* 強調「在地共好」與「跨世代參與」，示範小鎮如何透過創新產業鏈重啟生命力。
  The case embodies “local co-prosperity” and “cross-generational participation,” showing how a town can revive via a new value chain.

## 二、【文字檔1】重要內容總結

Key summary of Text 1

1. 2019 年起，臺灣推動地方創生國家戰略，面對人口外流與城鄉失衡；即使遭遇 COVID-19 衝擊，各地創生團隊仍與社區緊密合作，展現韌性。
   Since 2019, Taiwan’s local revitalization strategy has tackled depopulation and inequality; despite COVID-19, local teams continued working closely with communities.

2. 雲林土庫鎮是高齡化嚴重、老街沒落的小鎮，但也是創生案例「土庫藝可可莊園」的舞台。
   Tuku in Yunlin is a heavily aging town with a declining old street, yet it became the stage for the “Tuku-Yi Cocoa Manor” revitalization case.

3. 創辦人陳銀豪為了照顧失智父親返鄉，買下前身為廢棄養豬場與地下毒場的污染農地，忍痛註銷價值高昂的養豬執照，投入巨額資金整治土地。
   Founder Chen returned home to care for his father, buying polluted land that was once a pig farm and drug site, cancelling a valuable license and investing heavily in remediation.

4. 他從零開始學農，選擇可可作為作物，結合父親的園藝治療需求與可可對失智、心血管的健康益處，打造全程天然、無添加、成本高但高品質的頂級巧克力。
   Starting from scratch, he chose cocoa for both therapeutic work and its health benefits, creating premium, additive-free chocolate with high production costs.

5. 土庫藝可可莊園採用智慧溫室與 Tree-to-Bar 模式，提供食農教育與觀光體驗，遊客可參與從栽種到製成巧克力的完整流程，園區在假日能吸引上千名遊客。
   The manor uses smart greenhouses and a tree-to-bar model, offering educational and tourism experiences where visitors see the full cocoa process; thousands come on weekends.

6. 透過可可莊園的亮點效應，帶動土庫老街人潮回流，促成返鄉青年與老店二、三代組成商圈協會，串聯農會、觀光工廠、在地品牌，形成完整的觀光產業鏈。
   The cocoa estate’s “flagship effect” draws people back to Tuku Old Street, encouraging youth and shop successors to form a business association and link multiple local brands into a tourism chain.

7. 此案例展示地方創生不只是產業創新，更包括：土地修復、家族照顧、人口返鄉、社區組織與跨世代合作，成為雲林小鎮翻轉與「在地共好」的典型故事。
   This case shows that local revitalization is not only about industry innovation but also land healing, family care, return migration, community organizing, and cross-generational cooperation.
